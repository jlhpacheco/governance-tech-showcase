# Enterprise Workflow Architecture

## Objective

Design operational systems that support secure access, workflow management, auditability, reporting, and analytics.

## High-Level Architecture

Users  
↓  
Web Application  
↓  
Business Services  
↓  
Data Layer  
↓  
Reporting and Analytics  

## Design Principles

- Separation of concerns
- Secure access
- Auditability
- Scalability
- Reporting readiness
- Data-informed decision support
